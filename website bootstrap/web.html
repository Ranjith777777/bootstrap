The cost of living in Chennai is increasing every year. Paying rent for PGs, shared apartments, or small houses often consumes a large part of their salary. The average rent for a single room or small flat ranges from ₹7,000 to ₹10,000 per month, excluding electricity and maintenance charges

People living in Chennai, especially those above 21, face various health challenges due to their busy urban lifestyle. Long working hours, traffic stress, and irregular eating habits affect both physical and mental health

Mid-aged people in Chennai, especially working professionals and students living away from home, face several food-related problems due to their busy lifestyle. Most people depend on hotels, roadside eateries, or online food delivery instead of home-cooked meals
